## Why retrospectives

Retros help you turn _events_ into _insights_:

- What worked
- What didn't
- What we should try next

## Template

### Context

What was the goal, constraints, and scope?

### Outcomes

- Metrics moved
- Quality outcomes
- User feedback

### Learnings

Write 3-5 bullets you want to remember.
